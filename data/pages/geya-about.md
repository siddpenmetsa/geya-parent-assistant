GEYA stands for Glenmoore Eagle Youth Association. GEYA is a nonprofit, volunteer organization.

GEYA provides Recreational Soccer, HIGH 5 Soccer, Little League Baseball, and Junior American Legion Baseball programs for children in Kindergarten through High School during all seasons of the year.

GEYA's mission is to pursue excellence in athletics while making teamwork, good sportsmanship, and friendship the goal of all actions. GEYA uses the collective talents of its members to promote confidence, goodwill, and leadership.
