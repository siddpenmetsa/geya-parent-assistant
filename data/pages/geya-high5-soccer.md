HIGH 5 Soccer is GEYA's fun soccer program for players with special needs. Once a week, players pair with a teen or adult buddy for soccer-based activities and games adapted to their abilities.

HIGH 5 focuses on teamwork, self-esteem, sportsmanship, respect, and fun.

HIGH 5 players can be ages 5 to 18. Buddies are usually in middle school or high school. Younger children who want to volunteer as buddies should contact the HIGH 5 coaches at HIGH5@geyasoccer.org. Buddies over 18 must complete the background checks required by Pennsylvania.

HIGH 5 sessions are on Sundays, September 13 to November 8. All HIGH 5 sessions take place at Fellowship Field #1, 240 Fellowship Road, Chester Springs.

Buddy training for new and returning buddies is Wednesday, August 26 at 7:00 PM at The Barn at Upland Farm, 301 Pottstown Pike, Chester Springs. Entrance and parking are on Darrell Drive.

HIGH 5 players should bring a water bottle and wear sneakers and comfortable clothes. Players receive a HIGH 5 t-shirt on the first day and should wear it to every session. Cleats and shin guards are optional. GEYA provides juice and a snack after the session.

HIGH 5 has no cost. It is free to all participants thanks to donations and GEYA Soccer.

HIGH 5 families can expect a fun and meaningful experience, adapted games and activities, a safe and healthy environment, and opportunities to network with other families.

HIGH 5 parents need to provide transportation to and from all sessions, stay on the sidelines during sessions, and lend support in a positive manner.

If a player is new to HIGH 5, Coach Matt will call before the season starts to introduce himself and answer questions.

A player is a participant with special needs. A buddy is a teenager or adult who works one-on-one with a player during HIGH 5 sessions.

HIGH 5 does not have a residency requirement. Children can participate no matter where they live.

People can help HIGH 5 as a buddy, an activity leader, or a snack helper. Volunteers do not need soccer experience as long as they are enthusiastic, patient, and love helping kids smile.

Donations can be mailed by check to GEYA Soccer, P.O. Box 164, Uwchland PA 19480, with "HIGH 5" in the notes section, or brought to a session.

HIGH 5 Soccer began in September 2012 with 7 players and 12 buddies. HIGH 5 is based on US Youth Soccer's TOPSoccer model.
