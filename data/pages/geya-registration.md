GEYA registration is handled online through program-specific registration links.

Little League Baseball registration is handled through the GEYA Baseball main site.

Spring Soccer registration for 2026 was posted on February 21, 2026. The Spring Soccer registration deadline is March 7, 2026.

2026 Spring Soccer registration dates:
- Early Bird Registration: December 15 to January 31.
- Regular Registration: February 1 to February 28.
- Late Registration: March 1 to March 7.

2026 Fall Soccer registration dates:
- Early Bird Registration: June 1 to June 30.
- Regular Registration: July 1 to July 31.
- Late Registration: August 1 to August 15.

HIGH 5 Soccer player and buddy registration is available through TeamSnap.

Junior American Legion Baseball has a 2024 TeamSnap registration form listed in the current indexed GEYA resources.
