GEYA has a Junior American Legion Baseball program.

The American Legion Baseball page lists a 2024 registration link and TeamSnap form for Junior American Legion Baseball.

Pennsylvania American Legion Baseball includes players ages 13-19. Pennsylvania American Legion Baseball uses wood bats only.

American Legion Baseball aims to prepare players for future success in baseball and in life. The program emphasizes teamwork, team loyalty, discipline, leadership, and good sportsmanship.

American Legion Code of Sportsmanship:
- Keep the rules.
- Keep faith with teammates.
- Keep temper.
- Keep myself fit.
- Keep a stout heart in defeat.
- Keep pride under in victory.
- Keep a sound soul, clean mind, and healthy body.
